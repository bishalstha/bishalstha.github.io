Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information that is received and collected by our app and how it is used.

Information Collection and Use

Our app does not collect, store, or share any personal information. We do not require you to provide any personal information to use the app.

Our app may request access to certain features of your phone such as storage access. These features are used solely to provide the functionality of the app and are not used to collect any data.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses Sentry to collect crash reports and performance data to help diagnose and fix errors. Sentry may collect technical information such as error stack traces, device model, OS and app version, and limited network information (for example, an IP address). We do not intentionally collect or send personal user content to Sentry. Collected data is used solely to improve app stability and performance and is processed under Sentry's terms. For details, see Sentry's privacy policy: https://sentry.io/privacy/.
